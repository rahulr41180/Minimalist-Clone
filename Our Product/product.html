What it is: A powerful peeling trio of 25% AHA, 5% PHA & 2% BHA that provides multi-level exfoliation, both on the surface & inside the pores, giving you a healthy, plump, and glowing skin.

  • A whopping 25% AHA, 5% PHA & 2% BHA blend formulated at a pH of 3.4 - 3.8 for maximum effectiveness. AHA & PHA exfoliates while BHA clears congestion of pore, delivering incredible brightening & elastic feel for the baby-soft complexion
  • PHAs are next generation AHAs. Not just an exfoliator, PHAs are also humectants and moisturizers. Further, Gluconolactone (PHA used in this formula) provides up to 50% protection against UV radiation
